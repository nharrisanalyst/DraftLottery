Draft Lottery

Pick which team wins the lottery to see how the odds change depending on which team wins. After the top three pick are made the teams are then order into the order in which the will draft.

*-SAC's pick swaps with Philly if it is behind Philly's pick.
-Sac's pick goes to Chicago if out of the top ten.
-LAL pick goes to Philly if not in the top three.
-Boston's draft odds are calculated by the fact they own Brooklyn's pick.
